## Important correction
The earlier schedule mixed a few paths. The safest consistent structure is:
- `features/.../domain` for entities, repositories, and use cases.
- `features/.../data` for DTOs and repository implementations.
- `features/.../presentation` for blocs, pages, and widgets.
- `app/` for DI and app bootstrap.
- `core/` and `shared/` for reusable app-wide utilities.[2][4][1]

One more correction: `app_router.dart` should be inside `lib/app/router/app_router.dart`, not under `lib/presentation/router/`.[3][5]
